the process
We know that branding your business can feel overhelming. That’s why we break the process down into 3 steps.





The discovery phase is the initial step in our journey. This is where we, me and you, will hammer out all the objectives and find a solution that best suits your needs. At this stage we will identify your mission, vision and goals that best suit your brand.

This process will enable us to hit the ground running, giving us a solid foundation that starts with getting to know you and your brand. Depending on which package you decide to go for, we will then ask you to complete a questionnaire which will enable us to have a clear vision as to what the full scope of the project is. There may be a little homework involved for you, but don't worry we promise it'll be fun!






After all the nitty gritty details are out of the way, here comes the exciting part! Once you have approved the brand strategy, we begin to create multiple concepts, which will then be presented to you in an in-depth concept presentation. Once presented, we provide revisions as per your notes and on to the next!






Here we go! As our journey comes to an end, we begin to discuss our launch strategy. All the necessary material will be sent to you with a brand standards manual clearly stating how best to implement your unique, upgraded branding. This material will serve as a guidepost for future brand decisions. And now, it's time to launch. Congratulations!

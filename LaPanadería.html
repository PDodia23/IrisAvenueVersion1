La Panadería - Bakery

La Panadería is a bakery and cafe based in San Francisco. They approached us with an open mind and were happy for us to recreate their branding from the ground up.

Our initial discovery call with La Panadería enabled us to get a firm idea as to how the company would want to present themselves. Our collective aim was to create something that exhibits simplicity with a touch of elegance. La Panadería wanted us to create their new logo and packaging for their baked goods.

Branding Guidelines
Elegant & Simple

Our goal for La Panadería was to create a brand that feels elegant yet simple. We did this by combining elevated & strong typography with a clean logo.

In terms of the colour palette we chose for La Panadería, we decided to go down the bold route. With a simple yet effective logo, we decided to pair it with an eye catching colour palette with gold accents represented via the design on the packaging.
